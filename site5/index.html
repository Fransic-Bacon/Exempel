Our Story

Welcome to Mario's Pizza Palace, where tradition meets taste! Since 1952, our family has been crafting authentic Italian pizzas using recipes passed down through three generations.

We source our ingredients directly from Italy - San Marzano tomatoes, fresh mozzarella, and the finest olive oil. Our dough is made fresh daily and hand-tossed to perfection by our master pizza makers.

What started as a small neighborhood pizzeria has grown into a beloved local institution, but we've never forgotten our roots. Every pizza that leaves our kitchen carries the same passion and attention to detail that Nonna Maria put into her very first pie.

Come taste the difference that three generations of pizza-making expertise makes!
